Vanilla flavoured donut

Desert, American Sweets

Ingredients Used

  1. All-purpose flour - 2 cups (about 250 grams)
  2. Granulated sugar - ½ cup(about 100 grams)
  3. Baking powder - 1 ½ teaspoons (about 7 grams)
  4. Salt - ½ teaspoon (about 2.5 grams)
  5. Milk - ¾ cup (about 180 milliliters)
  6. Egg- 1 large
  7. Unsalted butter, melted - 2 tablespoons (about 30 grams)
  8. Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon (about 5 milliliters)

Calorie count: 240 kcal

Protein : 2g/serving
